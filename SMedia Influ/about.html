About Us

SMedia Influ is an influencer marketing business that was founded a year ago by two young people who were still in college at the time. We are pleased to announce the beginning of our second year of operation. SMedia Influ is now fully functional, thanks to our top staff and the members of our creative team. Although its primary focus is on influencer marketing, SMedia Influ also provides the aforementioned services to its clients. In the not too distant future, we plan to provide an expanded range of services. Within the realm of influencer marketing, we hope to usher in a new period of revolutionary change within the next generation. For more information, be sure to follow us on all of our social media sites.
